# Machinery Fault Response Playbook

Use this playbook when the detector predicts fault-like behavior, sensor/process features dominate the explanation, or the anomaly is consistent with physical degradation rather than network manipulation.

## Signals

- Gradual drift in temperature, vibration, pressure, flow, current, or load
- Sensor readings inconsistent with normal operating envelope
- Repeated abnormality under similar operating conditions
- No strong evidence of suspicious network activity

## Immediate Actions

1. Inspect the affected machine, sensor, or actuator.
2. Check calibration and recent maintenance history.
3. Compare readings with redundant sensors or manual measurement.
4. Reduce load or switch to safe operating mode if needed.
5. Notify the maintenance or reliability engineering team.

## Escalation

Treat as high severity if the fault may cause safety risk, production loss, or cascading equipment damage.
